Tier 3 — Back-end

Sinh mã & tối ưu: IR, optimization, bytecode VM, control flow, register allocation.

Tier này dạy gì?

  • IR (Three-Address): mã trung gian phẳng, SSA, basic block & CFG.
  • Optimization: constant folding/propagation, dead code, CSE, algebraic.
  • Bytecode & Stack VM: biên dịch xuống lệnh phẳng, chạy trên máy ảo ngăn xếp.
  • Control flow codegen: if/while → label & jump, backpatching, CFG.
  • Register allocation & Targets: liveness, graph coloring, spill; native/WASM/JVM.
