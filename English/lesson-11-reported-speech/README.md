# Lesson 11 — Lời nói gián tiếp (Reported Speech)

> Chuyển từ lời trực tiếp `"I am tired"` thành lời gián tiếp `She said (that) she was tired`. Khâu cuối của Nhánh II Ngữ pháp — kết hợp toàn bộ kiến thức về thì.

## Mục tiêu học tập

1. Nắm **backshift rule**: khi reporting verb ở quá khứ, các thì trong câu được "lùi 1 bước".
2. Báo cáo được **3 loại câu**: statements (khẳng định), questions (Yes/No + Wh-), commands/requests (mệnh lệnh).
3. Biết các **trường hợp KHÔNG backshift**: chân lý, sự thật còn đúng, reporting verb ở hiện tại.
4. Dùng đúng **reporting verbs**: say vs tell, ask vs request, advise vs suggest...

## 1. Lời trực tiếp vs gián tiếp — Đặt vấn đề

**Direct speech** (trực tiếp): trích nguyên văn lời người nói, đặt trong dấu nháy.

```
Mary said, "I am tired."
            ↑
       trích chính xác lời Mary nói
```

**Reported speech** (gián tiếp / indirect speech): kể lại lời người khác, không trích nguyên văn.

```
Mary said (that) she was tired.
                  ↑       ↑
              đổi I→she  lùi am→was (backshift)
```

### 3 thay đổi cần làm khi chuyển

| | Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. **Đại từ** (pronoun) | I, you, my, your, we... | she/he, they, his/her... |
| 2. **Thì** (tense) | hiện tại, tương lai | "lùi" về quá khứ (backshift) |
| 3. **Thời gian/nơi chốn** (time/place markers) | now, today, here, this | then, that day, there, that |

Mỗi thay đổi có quy luật riêng — học từng nhóm.

---

## 2. Backshift Rule — Lùi thì khi reporting verb ở QK

Khi **reporting verb** (verb dùng để báo cáo: said, told, asked...) ở **quá khứ**, các thì trong câu được lùi 1 bước:

### Bảng backshift đầy đủ

| Direct (ban đầu) | Reported (sau backshift) |
| --- | --- |
| **Present Simple** — *"I work"* | **Past Simple** — *...that he worked* |
| **Present Continuous** — *"I am working"* | **Past Continuous** — *...that he was working* |
| **Present Perfect** — *"I have worked"* | **Past Perfect** — *...that he had worked* |
| **Past Simple** — *"I worked"* | **Past Perfect** — *...that he had worked* |
| **Past Continuous** — *"I was working"* | **Past Perfect Continuous** — *...that he had been working* |
| **Past Perfect** — *"I had worked"* | **Past Perfect** (giữ nguyên — đã lùi tối đa) |
| **will** — *"I will work"* | **would** — *...that he would work* |
| **can** — *"I can swim"* | **could** — *...that he could swim* |
| **may** — *"I may go"* | **might** — *...that he might go* |
| **must** — *"I must go"* | **had to** — *...that he had to go* |
| **shall** — *"I shall return"* | **should** | (hiếm dùng) |

**Modal không thay đổi**: `could`, `would`, `should`, `might`, `ought to` — đã lùi rồi.

### Ví dụ chi tiết

**Direct**:
> Mary said, "I am studying English."

**Reported** (apply backshift):
- `am studying` (Present Continuous) → `was studying` (Past Continuous)
- `I` → `she`

> *Mary said (that) **she was studying** English.*

**Một loạt ví dụ khác**:

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He said, "I work in a bank." | He said (that) **he worked** in a bank. |
| She said, "I have finished my homework." | She said (that) **she had finished** her homework. |
| Tom said, "I will call you tomorrow." | Tom said (that) **he would call** me **the next day**. |
| They said, "We can swim." | They said (that) **they could swim**. |
| He said, "I am tired now." | He said (that) **he was tired then**. |

## 3. Khi nào KHÔNG backshift?

Có 3 trường hợp giữ nguyên thì:

### 3.1 Reporting verb ở HIỆN TẠI / TƯƠNG LAI

Khi chính reporting verb ở present hoặc future, không cần lùi thì câu được báo cáo.

```
Direct:    Mary says, "I am tired."
Reported:  Mary says (that) she is tired.   ← KHÔNG lùi, vì 'says' ở present
```

```
Direct:    Tom will say, "I'm coming."
Reported:  Tom will say (that) he is coming.   ← KHÔNG lùi
```

### 3.2 Chân lý / Sự thật vĩnh viễn

Sự thật khoa học, quy luật chung — KHÔNG cần lùi vì vẫn đúng đến hiện tại.

```
Direct:    The teacher said, "The Earth revolves around the Sun."
Reported:  The teacher said (that) the Earth revolves around the Sun.
           (Trái Đất vẫn đang quay → không lùi sang 'revolved')
```

```
Direct:    Mom said, "Water boils at 100°C."
Reported:  Mom said (that) water boils at 100°C.
```

### 3.3 Việc vẫn đúng tới giờ

Nếu việc được báo cáo **vẫn còn đúng** lúc kể, có thể giữ nguyên thì:

```
Direct:    He said, "I live in Hanoi." (5 phút trước, anh ấy vẫn ở Hanoi)
Reported:  He said (that) he lives in Hanoi.   ← KHÔNG lùi, vì vẫn đúng
```

Hoặc lùi cũng được:
> He said (that) **he lived** in Hanoi.

→ Cả hai đều đúng. Lùi: tập trung vào "lúc đó". Không lùi: nhấn vẫn đúng đến giờ.

## 4. Chuyển đổi từ/cụm thời gian và nơi chốn

| Direct (lúc đó) | Reported (kể lại) |
| --- | --- |
| now | then / at that time |
| today | that day |
| tonight | that night |
| yesterday | the day before / the previous day |
| tomorrow | the next day / the following day |
| last week | the previous week / the week before |
| next week | the following week / the week after |
| ago | before |
| here | there |
| this (chỉ định) | that |
| these | those |

### Ví dụ áp dụng

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He said, "I'll call you **tomorrow**." | He said (that) he would call me **the next day**. |
| She said, "I bought it **yesterday**." | She said (that) she had bought it **the day before**. |
| Tom said, "**This** book is mine." | Tom said (that) **that** book was his. |
| Mary said, "I love living **here**." | Mary said (that) she loved living **there**. |

## 5. Báo cáo 3 loại câu

### 5.1 Statements (khẳng định) — đã làm ở §2

Pattern: `S + reporting verb (+ that) + reported clause`

`that` có thể bỏ trong văn nói:
- *He said (that) he was tired.*
- *She told me (that) she would come.*

### 5.2 Yes/No questions

Pattern: `S + asked + (object) + if/whether + S + V (backshift)`

**Lưu ý**: Đảo lại trật tự về chủ ngữ + verb như câu khẳng định (không còn dạng câu hỏi).

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He asked, "Are you tired?" | He asked **if/whether** I was tired. |
| She asked, "Do you speak English?" | She asked **if/whether** I spoke English. |
| Tom asked, "Have you finished?" | Tom asked **if/whether** I had finished. |
| They asked, "Will you come?" | They asked **if/whether** I would come. |

**KHÔNG dùng** dấu chấm hỏi ở cuối câu reported (không còn là câu hỏi):
- ❌ *He asked if I was tired?*
- ✅ *He asked if I was tired.*

### 5.3 Wh-questions

Pattern: `S + asked + (object) + wh-word + S + V (backshift)`

Giữ nguyên từ để hỏi (what, where, when, why, how, who...), bỏ `do/does/did`, đảo về thứ tự chủ ngữ-verb.

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He asked, "**What** do you want?" | He asked **what** I wanted. |
| She asked, "**Where** do you live?" | She asked **where** I lived. |
| Tom asked, "**Why** are you crying?" | Tom asked **why** I was crying. |
| Mary asked, "**How** did you do it?" | Mary asked **how** I had done it. |

### 5.4 Commands / Requests (mệnh lệnh)

Pattern: `S + told/asked/ordered + object + to + V (base)`

**Quan trọng**: dùng `to + V` (không backshift verb chính).

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| The teacher said, "**Open** your books." | The teacher told us **to open** our books. |
| Mom said, "**Don't** play in the rain." | Mom told me **not to play** in the rain. |
| He said, "Please **call** me later." | He asked me **to call** him later. |
| She said, "**Go** away!" | She ordered me **to go** away. |

**Phủ định**: `not + to + V`.

## 6. Reporting verbs — Dùng từ nào?

`said` và `told` là phổ biến nhất, nhưng tiếng Anh có nhiều reporting verb với sắc thái riêng.

### 6.1 `say` vs `tell`

| | **say** | **tell** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Có người nhận? | KHÔNG kèm object (người nghe) | BẮT BUỘC kèm object (người nghe) |
| Pattern | *He **said** that...* | *He **told me** that...* |
| Ví dụ | *She said she was tired.* | *She told me she was tired.* |

**Lỗi phổ biến**:
- ❌ *He told that he was tired.* (thiếu object)
- ✅ *He told me that he was tired.* HOẶC *He said that he was tired.*

### 6.2 `ask` — Dùng cho câu hỏi & yêu cầu lịch sự

- *He asked me **if** I was tired.* (câu hỏi)
- *She asked me **to help** her.* (yêu cầu)

### 6.3 Các reporting verbs khác (sắc thái)

| Verb | Sắc thái | Pattern | Ví dụ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `advise` | khuyên | advise sb + to V | He **advised** me **to study** harder. |
| `warn` | cảnh báo | warn sb + (not) to V | She **warned** me **not to go** alone. |
| `suggest` | đề xuất | suggest + V-ing / that + S + V | He **suggested going** to the beach. / He **suggested that we go**. |
| `offer` | đề nghị | offer + to V | She **offered to help** me. |
| `promise` | hứa | promise + to V | He **promised to come**. |
| `agree` | đồng ý | agree + to V | They **agreed to meet**. |
| `refuse` | từ chối | refuse + to V | She **refused to answer**. |
| `complain` | phàn nàn | complain + that + S + V | He **complained that** the food was cold. |
| `admit` | thừa nhận | admit + V-ing / that + S + V | She **admitted lying**. / She **admitted that** she had lied. |
| `deny` | phủ nhận | deny + V-ing | He **denied stealing** the money. |

## 7. Bài tập

### Bài 7.1 — Chuyển sang reported speech (statements)

1. He said, "I am hungry."
2. She said, "I bought a new car yesterday."
3. They said, "We will travel to Paris next month."
4. Tom said, "I have lived here for 10 years."
5. Mary said, "It is raining heavily now."

### Bài 7.2 — Báo cáo câu hỏi

1. He asked, "Are you a student?"
2. She asked, "Where do you live?"
3. Tom asked me, "When will the meeting start?"
4. Mary asked, "Have you seen this movie?"
5. He asked, "Why didn't you call me?"

### Bài 7.3 — Báo cáo mệnh lệnh

1. The teacher said, "Close your books."
2. Mom said, "Don't touch the stove."
3. He said, "Please help me with this."
4. She said, "Wait for me here."
5. Doctor said, "Take this medicine twice a day."

### Bài 7.4 — Tìm và sửa lỗi

1. *He told that he was tired.*
2. *She asked me when did I come.*
3. *Tom said the Earth revolved around the Sun.*
4. *Mary asked me to don't go there.*
5. *He said me that he was happy.*

### Bài 7.5 — Có nên backshift?

Quyết định backshift hay không + giải thích:

1. The teacher says, "English is a global language."
2. He said yesterday, "Water boils at 100°C."
3. She said an hour ago, "I am hungry." (giờ chắc vẫn đói)
4. He said 5 years ago, "I work in Hanoi." (giờ không còn ở Hanoi nữa)

## 8. Lời giải chi tiết

### Lời giải 7.1

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He said, "I am hungry." | He said **(that) he was hungry**. |
| She said, "I bought a new car yesterday." | She said **(that) she had bought** a new car **the day before**. |
| They said, "We will travel to Paris next month." | They said **(that) they would travel** to Paris **the following month**. |
| Tom said, "I have lived here for 10 years." | Tom said **(that) he had lived there for 10 years**. |
| Mary said, "It is raining heavily now." | Mary said **(that) it was raining heavily then**. |

### Lời giải 7.2

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| He asked, "Are you a student?" | He asked **if/whether I was a student**. |
| She asked, "Where do you live?" | She asked **where I lived**. (bỏ 'do', đảo về S+V) |
| Tom asked me, "When will the meeting start?" | Tom asked me **when the meeting would start**. |
| Mary asked, "Have you seen this movie?" | Mary asked **if/whether I had seen that movie**. |
| He asked, "Why didn't you call me?" | He asked **why I hadn't called him**. |

### Lời giải 7.3

| Direct | Reported |
| --- | --- |
| The teacher said, "Close your books." | The teacher **told us to close** our books. |
| Mom said, "Don't touch the stove." | Mom **told me not to touch** the stove. |
| He said, "Please help me with this." | He **asked me to help** him with that. |
| She said, "Wait for me here." | She **told me to wait** for her there. |
| Doctor said, "Take this medicine twice a day." | Doctor **told me to take** that medicine twice a day. |

### Lời giải 7.4

| # | Sai | Đúng | Vì sao |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | He **told** that he was tired. | He **said** that he was tired. (hoặc *He told **me** that he was tired*) | 'tell' bắt buộc có object |
| 2 | She asked me **when did I come**. | She asked me **when I came** (hoặc had come). | Reported question không dùng cấu trúc câu hỏi (không 'did') |
| 3 | Tom said the Earth **revolved** around the Sun. | Tom said the Earth **revolves** around the Sun. | Chân lý — KHÔNG backshift |
| 4 | Mary asked me **to don't** go there. | Mary asked me **not to** go there. | Phủ định: not + to V |
| 5 | He **said me** that he was happy. | He **told me** that he was happy. (hoặc *He **said to me*** that...) | 'say' không kèm object trực tiếp |

### Lời giải 7.5

| Câu | Backshift? | Vì sao |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The teacher **says**, "English is a global language." | **KHÔNG** | Reporting verb (says) ở hiện tại → không lùi |
| He said yesterday, "Water boils at 100°C." | **KHÔNG** (giữ 'boils') | Chân lý khoa học — luôn đúng |
| She said an hour ago, "I am hungry." (giờ chắc vẫn đói) | **CÓ THỂ KHÔNG** | Việc vẫn đúng → cả hai cách (*she is hungry* / *she was hungry*) đều chấp nhận |
| He said 5 years ago, "I work in Hanoi." (giờ không còn) | **CÓ** | Đã thay đổi → lùi sang `worked` để chỉ thời điểm đã qua |
