SNS Pathway (nhanh ~giây)

Hypothalamus → SNS → Adrenal Medulla → Epinephrine + Norepinephrine.
Tức thì: HR↑, BP↑, đồng tử giãn.

HPA Pathway (chậm ~phút)

Hypothalamus → CRH → Pituitary → ACTH → Adrenal Cortex → Cortisol.
Sustained: glucose↑, immune suppress (short-term).
