AI-ML

Học máy & AI — 5 tier × 20 bài, từ pipeline cơ bản qua classical methods (tree/SVM/boosting), unsupervised, deep learning (CNN/RNN/Transformer) tới NLP & multimodal applications.

Lĩnh vực này dạy gì?

  • Foundations: pipeline, linear/logistic regression, bias-variance & regularization.
  • Classical methods: KNN, decision tree, random forest, SVM, gradient boosting — vẫn dominate tabular data.
  • Unsupervised: K-means, DBSCAN, PCA/SVD, t-SNE/UMAP.
  • Deep learning: MLP training deep, CNN, RNN/LSTM, Transformer + attention.
  • NLP & multimodal: text vectorization, word embeddings, vector DB + RAG, CLIP.
