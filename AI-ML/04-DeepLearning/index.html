Tier 4 — Deep Learning

Neural network deep dive: MLP training, CNN, RNN/LSTM, Transformer. Đi từ backprop đến self-attention.

Tier này dạy gì?

  • MLP & backprop deep: optimizers (SGD/momentum/Adam), batchnorm, weight init, vanishing/exploding.
  • CNN: convolution, pooling, receptive field; LeNet → AlexNet → ResNet.
  • RNN/LSTM: sequence modeling, vanishing gradient, gates; seq2seq.
  • Transformer: self-attention (Q/K/V), multi-head, positional encoding.
