Tier 3 — Systems & Architecture

Hệ thống & kiến trúc: particle, constraint (cloth/rope), flocking, ECS, tilemap & A*.

Tier này dạy gì?

  • Particle systems: emitter, lực, lifetime, object pool (khói/lửa/nước).
  • Constraints: Verlet + distance constraint — dây, vải, ragdoll.
  • Flocking & steering: boids 3 luật, steering behaviors, emergent behavior.
  • ECS & architecture: Entity-Component-System, state machine, camera.
  • Tilemap & pathfinding: lưới ô, đồ thị, A* tìm đường.

Cần Tier 2 — Collision trước. Liên hệ Algorithms (A*), DataStructures (graph, quadtree).
